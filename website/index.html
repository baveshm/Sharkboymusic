About

The Energy Behind
Every Event

Xavier Tan — known as DJ Sharkboy — is a professional DJ, MC, and event host based in Singapore, with 6 years of open format DJing spanning pop, progressive house, techno, and hardstyle.

Beyond the decks, Sharkboy owns every segment of your event — from intimate dinner sets and VIP introductions to full-crowd raves and corporate galas. His ability to read a room makes him as effective in a boardroom as he is in a club.
